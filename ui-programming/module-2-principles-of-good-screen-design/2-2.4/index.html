Content Ordering & List Design Laboratory

Welcome to the Content Ordering Simulation Lab. In this laboratory, you will study how to order, group, and structure interface lists using Human-Centered Design principles. You will prioritize dashboard elements, map alignment strategies, group mixed items, and perform a Capstone UX Audit on a broken interface.

1. Prioritize Establish visual hierarchy by organizing items based on importance.
2. Group & Chunk Reduce cognitive load by combining related items under header sections.
3. Evaluate Identify weak layout hierarchies and inspect dashboard usability defects.
