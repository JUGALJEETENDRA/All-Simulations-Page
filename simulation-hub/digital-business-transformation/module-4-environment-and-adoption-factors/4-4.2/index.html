📘 Strategic Reference Board: Consumer Adoption Behavior (Module 4.2)

1. Rogers' Adopter Cohorts: Innovation diffusion maps consumer groups: Innovators, Early Adopters, Early Majority, Late Majority, and Laggards. Winning mainstream segments requires crossing the Chasm.

2. Adoption Stages: Customers navigate through stages from first learning about an app (Awareness) to final integration (Adoption).

3. Key Innovation Factors: Diffusion rates depend on Relative Advantage, Compatibility, Complexity, Trialability, and Observability.

4. Friction Barriers: Strategists scale launch campaigns to lower complexity and raise relative compatibility to speed up adoption rates.
