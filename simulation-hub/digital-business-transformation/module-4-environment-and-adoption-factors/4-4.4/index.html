📘 Strategic Reference Board: Regulations & Competitive Factors (Module 4.4)

1. Regulatory Compliance: Managing external rules (consumer protection, ad standards) ensures a business operates legally. Failures trigger audits, legal bans, or financial penalties.

2. Competitive Strategy: Tracking competitor price shifts and product launches helps managers design defensive actions to protect market share.

3. Economic Shocks: Volatile macroeconomics (interest rates, inflation) dictate cost of capital and consumer spend. Strategies must scale budgets to preserve runway.

4. Corporate Compliance Balance: Executives coordinate legal policy with market competition to achieve growth without overstepping regulatory boundaries.
