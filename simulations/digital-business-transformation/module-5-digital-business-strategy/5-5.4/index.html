Integrated Communications Key Principles:
  • Customer Centricity: Selecting digital channels based on customer profiles rather than platform popularity.
  • RACE Lifecycle: Structuring content and media formats according to the customer's decision journey stage.
  • Omnichannel Consistency: Ensuring messages and touchpoints coordinate seamlessly across Paid, Owned, and Earned frameworks.
🏛️ Director Strategy Reference Manual
Digital Channel Strategy (Chaffey):

Strategy involves selecting optimal touchpoint channels (Website, Search, Email, Social, Mobile, Content, Affiliate) matching specific user segments, ensuring communication objectives are supported coherently.

Paid, Owned, and Earned Media:

- Paid: Advertising media bought to drive traffic (PPC Google Ads, Influencer Sponsorship).
- Owned: Media properties owned directly by the firm (Website, Organic Instagram, Email lists).
- Earned: Free customer advocacy and peer promotions (Customer reviews, organic user sharing).

RACE Customer Journey:

Maps user interactions across Reach (traffic generation), Act (website interaction/content), Convert (checkout transactions), and Engage (loyalty systems and post-purchase database triggers).
