Concise definitions for digital infrastructure governance

Ref
Digital Governance: System of authority and control guiding digital infrastructure strategy.
Risk Management: Identifying and mitigating assets' exposure to digital business disruptions.
Compliance: Adhering to legal standards (e.g., GDPR, PCI-DSS) governing client privacy data.
Digital Trust: Customer confidence in an organization's system security and reliability.
Information Security: Protecting digital data assets from unauthorized access or modification.
Business Continuity: Plans ensuring critical operations survive major datacenter outages.
Confidentiality: Ensuring data records are accessible only to authorized credentials.
Integrity: Safeguarding database records from unauthorized alterations.
Availability: Guaranteeing systems remain responsive and accessible to buyers.
Governance Controls: Policies and mechanisms (e.g., MFA, audits) enforcing compliance rules.
