PROJECT: STRATEGIC BRIEF
CLIENT: SMARTEDGE DIGITAL
DATE: JUN 2026

To: Business Strategy Analyst Team

Our client, SmartEdge, is a high-growth digital service platform planning a major expansion. However, before investing capital, their executive board has requested a rigorous strategic environmental analysis.

Your mission is to perform environmental scanning, evaluate consumer demands, benchmark key market competitors, and integrate multiple strategic inputs to recommend an aligned market-entry position.

This command console guides you through the strategic process across 3 analysis modules:

  • Level 1: SWOT Analysis Workshop – Categorize corporate evidence logs to map strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats, and formulate strategic options using a TOWS Matrix.
  • Level 2: Market & Competitor Analysis Studio – Evaluate customer demand segments alongside competitor profiles and identify competitive differentiation opportunities.
  • Level 3: Strategic Analysis Laboratory – Integrate SWOT data, demand parameters, and competitor insights to make definitive positioning recommendations across multiple corporate cases.

All environmental analysis layers must lock sequentially. You must resolve the SWOT and market analysis stages before entering the Strategic Analysis Laboratory.

                  SWOT & TOWS Framework (Dave Chaffey):

                  SWOT identifies internal Strengths/Weaknesses and external Opportunities/Threats. TOWS matches these inputs directly to formulate active growth or defense options.

                  Market & Competitor Analysis:

                  Evaluates market demographics (growth, size, adoption) alongside competitor profiles to identify target entry options that offer the highest differentiation potential.

                  Strategic Analysis Laboratory:

                  Applies integrated environmental scans to realistic corporate scenarios (bookstores, delivery platforms, regulatory healthcare hubs) to select defensible market positions.
