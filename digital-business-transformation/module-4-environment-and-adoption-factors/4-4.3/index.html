Simulation Challenge: Business Adoption of Digital Systems

📘 Strategic Reference Board: Business Adoption of Digital Systems (Module 4.3)

1. Digital Adoption Drivers: Businesses implement digital systems (ERP, CRM, eCommerce) to scale operational speed, satisfy customer demand, and defend competitive share.

2. Adoption Barriers: Implementation is constrained by high upfront investment, organizational inertia, process integration bottlenecks, and employee training gaps.

3. Change Management: Digital transformation succeeds when directors align internal staff habits with the technical capabilities of new systems.

4. Systems Governance: Managers oversee cost, speed, integration, and user adoption rates to ensure digital assets translate to sustainable returns.
