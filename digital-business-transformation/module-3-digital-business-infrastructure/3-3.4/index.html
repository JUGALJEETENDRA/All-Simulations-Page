Concise definitions for digital ecosystems

Ref
API: Standard contract allowing software platforms to securely exchange business data.
Web Service: Web-based interfaces exchanging structured data over standard Internet protocols.
Middleware: Software glue translating between legacy on-premise systems and modern web networks.
Hosting: Allocating hardware resources to run web applications and store databases.
Cloud Hosting: Dynamically-allocated virtual servers billed on utility usage metrics.
Digital Integration: Linking digital channels, CRM, and ERP to create unified customer flows.
Service Level Agreement (SLA): A business contract guaranteeing service uptime and defining financial penalties.
Digital Ecosystem: A collaborative network of customers, suppliers, and partners connected via APIs.
