1.5 Drivers and Barriers to E-Business Adoption

Adoption Parameters

Key Learning: E-Business Adoption Factors
Successful e-business adoption depends upon multiple organizational, technological, and business factors (such as Management Support, Competitive Pressure, and Customer Demand) rather than one single condition. Favorable infrastructure and manageable cost set a strong foundation for lift-off.

Key Learning: Trust in E-Business
Customer trust is a key requirement for successful e-business adoption. Trust depends on multidimensional factors (Security, Privacy, Reliability, Organizational Reputation) rather than a single feature like low pricing or website logos.
