To: Chief Value Officer (CVO)

Welcome to ValueHub. Currently, our company suffers from unfocused strategies, poorly defined goals, and unbalanced stakeholder value, resulting in declining customer satisfaction and rising churn.

Your mission is to completely restructure our business goals, align operational initiatives with stakeholders, and trace digital value creation stages using Dave Chaffey's textbook frameworks.

Your strategic workbook outlines three core focus levels:

  • Level 1: SMART Objective Studio – Reconstruct vague objectives into precise SMART targets (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound).
  • Level 2: Stakeholder Value Designer – Present realistic business initiatives and balance benefits, trade-offs, and justifications across Investors, Customers, Employees, Owners, Society, and Partners.
  • Level 3: Digital Value Creation Studio – Organize and connect digital processes (Customer Acquisition, Digital Marketing, Sales, CRM, Support, Continuous Improvement) with customer and business value objectives.

In accordance with leadership parameters, all stage workbooks are unlocked and active. You can browse through levels using the top navigation pills or the previous/next footer buttons at any time.

Value Creation Key Principles (Dave Chaffey):
  • SMART Objectives: Provides a measurable direction for digital strategy and supports long-term value.
  • Stakeholder Balancing: Healthy digital business model architectures balance benefits across all nodes, avoiding lopsided optimization.
  • Value Creation Chain: Upstream/downstream digital processes systematically add value to customers and lock in strategic advantages.
🏛️ Chief Value Officer Reference Manual
SMART Objectives (Chaffey):

Goals must be Specific (defining the operational target), Measurable (establishing numeric checks), Achievable (realistic implementation paths), Relevant (aligned with business needs), and Time-bound (securing a clear deadline).

Stakeholder Value Balancing:

Modern e-businesses must balance interests across Customers, Employees, Investors, Business Owners, Society, and Business Partners to secure a sustainable long-term model.

Digital Value Chain:

Rather than generic activities, value is generated via specialized digital processes including Customer Acquisition, Digital Marketing, Sales/Checkout, CRM database consolidation, Customer Support tools, and Continuous optimization metrics.
